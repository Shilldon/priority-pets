Your pet is my priority

Services

I provide pet-sitting, walking and visiting services to the West Berkshire area.

I offer pet-services that are tailored to your needs. From popping in and feeding your budgie or taking your dogs for a good walk I can work to your requirements.

I provide an individual dog walking service and do not walk groups of dogs (unless they are from the same home). This ensures that the dogs I walk are only in contact with other dogs with whom they are familiar.

I am also happy to pet sit cats, rabbits, guinea pigs, and others.
